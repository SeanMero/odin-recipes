Oatmeal

Description

A healthy and vegan bowl of oatmeal, inspired by Dr Michael Greger's cookbook How Not to Die but modified because some ingredients are hard to find where I live.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground flaxseed
  • 1/2 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2-3/4 cups of almond milk
  • OPTIONAL: 1-2 handfuls of mixed nuts, dried fruits, berries, and/or a spoonful of nut butter

Steps

  1. Add oats, flaxseed, chia seeds, cinnamon, and milk to a sealable container and mix them up
  2. Seal the container and leave it in the refrigerator overnight
  3. At breakfast time, add optional ingredients and enjoy
