Smoothie

Description

A vegan smoothie that is both healthy and delicious, from the cookbook "How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger of NutritionFacts.org

Ingredients

  • 1/2-1 cups of water
  • one or two bananas
  • 1/3 cups of frozen blueberries
  • one tablespoon of ground flaxseed
  • two tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • one tablespoon of almond butter
  • two cups of spinach leaves
  • 1-2 drops of vanilla extract
  • 1-2 soaked and pitted dates

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to food processor.
  2. Process until smoothly blended.
  3. Pour into cup and enjoy :)
