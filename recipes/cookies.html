Cookies

Description

Minimalist cookies made from healthy, vegan ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 bananas
  • 1 3/4 cups of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Mash bananas in a bowl
  4. Add oats and mix
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix
  6. Spoon cookie mixture onto parchment paper in whatever sizes you like.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Remove from oven. You should allow them to cool for a few minutes, but we both know you are going to try to eat one right away so we'd may as well add that to the instructions.
